Survey Participation Agreement

By completing this survey, you agree to have your responses included in an academic study investigating the relationship between the INPEX Europe office and INPEX Corporation.

  • The survey results will be treated anonymously, but due to a small sample size, absolute anonymity cannot be guaranteed.
  • You can choose whether or not to answer any questions; only answer those you are comfortable with.
  • Your participation is greatly appreciated and will contribute valuable insights to the research.
  • Click here if you would like to know more about the research and reasons for the questioning.

Section 1: Background Information

1. What is your role within the organization?
2. How long have you been working for the company?
3. Are you an expatriate (expat) or a local employee?
4. Where are you based?
5. What is your nationality?
6. How likely are you to stay with the company for the next 2 years?

Section 2: Organisational Culture

8. Do you feel that relationships and mutual trust are prioritized in your workplace?
9. Do you believe that your workplace focuses on sustainability and contributing to global communities?
10. Is there a strong emphasis on exploration, expansiveness, and creativity in your workplace?
11. Do you find that fun and excitement are integral parts of your workplace culture?
12. Is achieving goals and winning a significant focus in your workplace?
13. Do you feel that strength, decisiveness, and boldness are valued in your workplace?
14. Is planning, caution, and preparedness emphasized in your workplace?
15. Do respect, structure, and shared norms play a crucial role in your workplace culture?

Section 3: Management Style

16. Do you think the organization relies heavily on formal chains of command for decision-making?
17. Do you feel strategic decisions come from Tokyo HQ (as opposed to being derived by a subsidiary)?

Section 4: Corporate Vision and Values

18. Are you clearly aware of the organisation’s vision and core values?
19. Do the organisation’s vision and values significantly influence your day-to-day activities or decision-making?
